About me

With over a decade of experience in music composition, I have created over 500 tracks, with my music featured in commercial games, such as Forge Squad, The Bar Fight VR and Gembryo. My passion for chiptune and retro game music drives my creative process, ensuring each piece resonates with nostalgia and modern innovation.

I've also provided music and SFX for various game jams: Fuel Burning II, Abyssal Ambush, Liquid Leap, to name the most recent ones. Next to composition and sound design, I implement audio routines in Game Engines like Unreal Engine 4, Unity and Godot.

Apart from working on games, I've released a game music pack together with Harvey Carman, three EPs, two solo albums, and three collab albums together with GerCR, dubbed our VoidÂ² project.

In addition, I perform as concert master, solo Bb and Eb clarinettist for the town orchestra of Wilhelmsburg and organise sectional rehearsals.

Services Offered

I specialize in game music composition, particularly chiptune in the style of the NES and Genesis. Additionally, I am skilled in creating retro MIDI arrangements, and unique 1-bit music.
I'm capable of implementing music and sound in many modern game engines myself, or provide support where needed.
